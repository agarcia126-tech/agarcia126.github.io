Football (Soccer)

Football, also called soccer, is the most popular sport in the world. Played by two teams of each having 11 players on a field with a ball.

Basic rules

  • Two teams of 11 players each
  • Game lasts 90 minutes split into two halves
  • Players score by getting the ball into the opponents goal
  • No hands allowed except the goalkeeper in his area

Famous Players

  • Lionel Messi
  • Cristiano Ronaldo
  • Neymar Junior
  • Kyliian Mbappe
